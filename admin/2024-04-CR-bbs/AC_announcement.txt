Data Integrity BBS Cryptosuites v1.0
W3C Candidate Recommendation Snapshot, 28 March 2024

This version:
   https://www.w3.org/TR/2024/CR-vc-di-bbs-20240404/
Latest published version:
   https://www.w3.org/TR/vc-di-bbs/

Abstract:
This specification describes a Data Integrity Cryptosuite for 
use when generating digital signatures using the BBS signature 
scheme. The Signature Suite utilizes BBS signatures to provide 
selective disclosure and unlinkable derived proofs.

Status of this document:
https://www.w3.org/TR/2024/CR-vc-di-bbs-20240404/#sotd
