[This announcement will be forwarded to W3C group chairs]

The Verifiable Credentials Working Group charter [1] is hereby extended until
31st December, 2024.

All Recommendation-track technical specifications, that the Working Group 
is chartered for[1], are now in CR phase, with a number of implementations 
already in place. This extension gives the time for the group to complete
the exit criteria for all these documents and publish the respective
formal Recommendations.

More information about the Verifiable Credentials Working Group can be found on its
summary page [2], including information about group Chairs,
Team Contacts and instructions for joining the group.

If you have any questions or need further information, please contact
Ivan Herman, VC WG Staff Contact, at ivan@w3.org.

This extension follows section 4.5 of the W3C Process Document:
https://www.w3.org/2023/Process-20231103/#charter-extension
 
Thank you,
